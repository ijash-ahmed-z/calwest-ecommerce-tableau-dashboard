# Key findings

- The model contains **251,431 order lines**, **184,375 distinct orders** and **12,246 customers** across 44 products and 48 states.
- Gross revenue totals approximately **$81.27 million**, with approximately **$44.17 million profit** and a **54.35% profit margin**.
- The average order value is approximately **$440.79**.
- **Very Berry Parfum** is the highest-revenue product at approximately **$4.88 million**.
- The **East** is the highest-revenue region at approximately **$22.10 million**.
- **Cat Dry Food** is the highest-revenue subcategory at approximately **$12.65 million**.
- Promotion-linked orders generated their highest monthly revenue share in **January 2021**, at approximately **47.49%**.

Values are independently calculated from the locally supplied case-study data. See the CSV files in `outputs/` for detailed results.
